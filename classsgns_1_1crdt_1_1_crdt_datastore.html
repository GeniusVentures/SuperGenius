Detailed Description

CrdtDatastore provides a replicated go-datastore (key-value store) implementation using Merkle-CRDTs built with IPLD nodes.

This Datastore is agnostic to how new MerkleDAG roots are broadcasted to the rest of replicas (Broadcaster component) and to how the IPLD nodes are made discoverable and retrievable to by other replicas (DAGSyncer component).

The implementation is based on the "Merkle-CRDTs: Merkle-DAGs meet CRDTs" paper by H�ctor Sanju�n, Samuli P�yht�ri and Pedro Teixeira.
